Convention of entering verbs: The verb should be entered with its anubandhas. e.g. zadx! for sad.
For verbs with upasargas preceding them, please remove Tu, Du, Ji. e.g. AkrIY for AG+qukrIY.
Also do satva and natva for sopasarga verbs. e.g. Asadx! for AG+zadx!, vinaSa! for vi+Rasa!.
For SLP1, use ! for anunAsika. e.g. bADf! for बाधृँ.
